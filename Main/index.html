About Me

My name is Tim Felbinger and I am a Computer Science Major at Georgia Institute of Technology. I was born in Germany and have lived in Williamsburg, Virginia most of my life.

I truly believe that video is the most powerful medium there is today for both capturing and sharing stories with the world.

Whether you want a timeless way to capture your wedding or a way to promote your organization, video is one of the best ways to do that. Video has a unique ability to fully immerse the viewer in the content and provide them with an enjoyable experience.

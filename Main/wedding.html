Base Package

Eight Hours of Coverage – This refers to the amount of time that is spent by our cinema team on location on any aspect of recording/ capturing media. Our team will capture the best possible video and audio throughout the time we are on location to make your wedding day that much more memorable. We find that eight hours is the amount of time that it takes for us to best capture the days events. You can request extra hours past the agreed upon coverage duration once we get verbal agreement the day of the wedding. We are happy to stay longer if you let us know.

Cinema Team – Depending on your wedding, we will have either one or two shooters at your wedding. We will be sure to capture your day from multiple angles and cameras, and produce high quality material to be used in your film. I (Tim Felbinger) act as the director of our cinema team on your wedding day and will also be the one editing your film. Same Day Film – The same day film is a 1-minute film that is finished before your wedding day is over. Film Options

Highlight Film – The highlight film is a 6-8 minute long overview of your wedding day. Includes audio mixing and color grading. This film will be delivered no later than 6 weeks after your wedding day. This is the most popular add-on film option.

Same Day Film – The same day film is a 1-minute film that is finished before your wedding day is over. This film is great for sharing your wedding day with all of your friends and family (even those who could not attend).

Documentary Edit - This film gives you fully edited multi-camera sequences of your entire ceremony, toasts, and dances. This is meant to be used as a way to go back and watch your romantic vows, hilarious toasts, dances, and any other moments that couldn’t be fit in your highlight film.

Additional Options

Extra Hours of Coverage – Add on additional hours of coverage by our cinema team.

Archive Hard Drive – A mirror image of all our raw footage on a portable hard drive for your archival. Allows for peace of mind that all your footage is safe in the event that anything were to happen. We only keep footage for one year after your wedding. Package sent via post.
